Team Zephyr

The app

The zephyr app is a way to mimick passing periods in schools or simply just to spark a conversation based on common interests. Because of the special time we are in right now, we though there would be a need to increase social interaction, especially because of the amount of time we spend on screens. Fill out the interest form to join the community and select your classes, so we can spark some conversations for you.
