The app

In this new world where socializing has become a challenge, how do we make connections with new people? And how can we reach out to others who have similar experiences and interests during the pandemic? We, the Zephyr team, created Zephyr, a web app that can connect students during social distancing and further support a sense of community in our schools. Based on our algorithm that looks at similar classes and interests, this app recommends users to reach out to others online and provides a way to contact them.
